Helmholtz_ph

Function to calculate analytic derivatives for computing d and t given p and h

Syntax

nderivs = Helmholtz_ph(f)

Inputs (1)

f

Type: HelmholtzDerivs

Description: Dimensionless derivatives of Helmholtz function

Outputs (1)

nderivs

Type: NewtonDerivatives_ph

Description: Derivatives for Newton iteration to calculate d and t from p and h

GasForce

Simple gas force computation for combustion engine

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

Very simple analytic model of the force generated by the combustion in an engine cylinder.

Parameters (5)

L

Value:

Type: Length (m)

Description: Length of cylinder
d

Value:

Type: Diameter (m)

Description: Diameter of cylinder
k0

Value: 0.01

Type: Volume (m³)

Description: Volume V = k0 + k1*(1-x), with x = 1 + s_rel/L
k1

Value: 1

Type: Volume (m³)

Description: Volume V = k0 + k1*(1-x), with x = 1 + s_rel/L
k

Value: 1

Type: HeatCapacity (J/K)

Description: Gas constant (p*V = k*T)

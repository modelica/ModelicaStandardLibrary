circular

Circular cross section

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

Function that returns the ThickEdgedOrifice.Geometry for a circular cross section of the orifice.

Syntax

geometry = circular(diameter, venaDiameter, venaLength)

Inputs (3)

diameter

Type: Diameter (m)

Description: Inner diameter of circular orifice
venaDiameter

Type: Diameter (m)

Description: Diameter of vena contraction
venaLength

Type: Length (m)

Description: Length of vena contraction

Outputs (1)

geometry

Type: Geometry

Description: Geometry of circular thick edged orifice

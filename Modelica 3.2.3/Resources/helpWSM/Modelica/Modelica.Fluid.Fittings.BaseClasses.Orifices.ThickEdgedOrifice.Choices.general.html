general

General cross section

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

Function that returns the ThickEdgedOrifice.Geometry for a general cross section of the orifice.

Syntax

geometry = general(crossArea, perimeter, venaCrossArea, venaPerimeter, venaLength)

Inputs (5)

crossArea

Type: Area (m²)

Description: Inner cross sectional area
perimeter

Type: Length (m)

Description: Inner perimeter
venaCrossArea

Type: Area (m²)

Description: Cross sectional area of vena contraction
venaPerimeter

Type: Length (m)

Description: Perimeter of vena contraction
venaLength

Type: Length (m)

Description: Length of vena contraction

Outputs (1)

geometry

Type: Geometry

Description: Geometry of circular thick edged orifice

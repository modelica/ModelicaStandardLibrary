quadraticPower

Quadratic power consumption characteristic

Syntax

consumption = quadraticPower(V_flow, V_flow_nominal, W_nominal)

Inputs (3)

V_flow

Type: VolumeFlowRate (m³/s)

Description: Volumetric flow rate
V_flow_nominal

Type: VolumeFlowRate[3] (m³/s)

Description: Volume flow rate for three operating points (single pump)
W_nominal

Type: Power[3] (W)

Description: Power consumption for three operating points

Outputs (1)

consumption

Type: Power (W)

Description: Power consumption

Extended by (1)

powerCharacteristic

Modelica.Fluid.Machines.BaseClasses.PartialPump

Power consumption vs. V_flow at nominal speed and density

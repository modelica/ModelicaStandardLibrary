massFraction_waterContent

Return mass fractions as a function of pressure, temperature and absolute humidity in kg(water)/kg(dry air)

Syntax

X = massFraction_waterContent(xw)

Inputs (1)

xw

Type: Real

Description: Water content in kg(water)/kg(dry air)

Outputs (1)

X

Type: MassFraction[:] (kg/kg)

Description: Mass fractions

FlCharacteristic

Pressure recovery characteristic

Syntax

rc = FlCharacteristic(pos)

Inputs (1)

pos

Type: Real

Description: Opening position (0: closed, 1: fully open)

Outputs (1)

rc

Type: Real

Description: Relative flow coefficient (per unit)

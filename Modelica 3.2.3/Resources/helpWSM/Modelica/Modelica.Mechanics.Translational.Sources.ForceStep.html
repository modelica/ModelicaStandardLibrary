ForceStep

Constant force, not dependent on speed

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

Model of a force step at time startTime. Positive force accelerates in positive direction of flange translation.

Parameters (4)

useSupport

Value: false

Type: Boolean

Description: = true, if support flange enabled, otherwise implicitly grounded
stepForce

Value:

Type: Force (N)

Description: Height of force step (if negative, force is acting as load)
offsetForce

Value:

Type: Force (N)

Description: Offset of force
startTime

Value: 0

Type: Time (s)

Description: Force = offset for time < startTime

Connectors (2)

flange

Type: Flange_b

Description: Flange of component
support

Type: Support

Description: Support/housing of component

Helmholtz_ps

Function to calculate analytic derivatives for computing d and t given p and s

Syntax

nderivs = Helmholtz_ps(f)

Inputs (1)

f

Type: HelmholtzDerivs

Description: Dimensionless derivatives of Helmholtz function

Outputs (1)

nderivs

Type: NewtonDerivatives_ps

Description: Derivatives for Newton iteration to compute d and t from p and s

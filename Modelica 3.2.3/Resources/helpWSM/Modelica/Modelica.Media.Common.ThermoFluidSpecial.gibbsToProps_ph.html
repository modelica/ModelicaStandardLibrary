gibbsToProps_ph

Calculate property record for pressure and specific enthalpy as states from dimensionless Gibbs function

Syntax

pro = gibbsToProps_ph(g)

Inputs (1)

g

Type: GibbsDerivs

Description: Dimensionless derivatives of Gibbs function

Outputs (1)

pro

Type: ThermoProperties_ph

Description: Property record for pressure and specific enthalpy as dynamic states

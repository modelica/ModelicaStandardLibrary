MixEntropy

Return mixing entropy of ideal gases / R

Syntax

smix = MixEntropy(x)

Inputs (1)

x

Type: MoleFraction[:]

Description: Mole fraction of mixture

Outputs (1)

smix

Type: Real

Description: Mixing entropy contribution, divided by gas constant

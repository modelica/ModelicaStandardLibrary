fid_R134a

Helmholtz coefficients of ideal part

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

This function computes the ideal gas helmholtz derivatives of the fundamental equation of Tillner-Roth and Baehr for R134a (1994) w.r.t. to reduced temperature (tau=T_crit/T) and reduced density (delta=rho/rho_crit).

Syntax

fid = fid_R134a(delta, tau)

Inputs (2)

delta

Type: Real

Description: Reduced density (delta=d/dcrit)
tau

Type: Real

Description: Reduced temperature (tau=Tcrit/T)

Outputs (1)

fid

Type: HelmholtzDerivs

Description: Helmholtz derivatives of ideal part

log

Natural (base e) logarithm (u shall be > 0)

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

This function returns y = log(10) (the natural logarithm of u), with u > 0:

Syntax

y = log(u)

Inputs (1)

u

Type: Real

Outputs (1)

y

Type: Real

drhol_dp

Derivative of density of saturated water w.r.t. pressure

Syntax

dd_dp = drhol_dp(p)

Inputs (1)

p

Type: Pressure (Pa)

Description: Saturation pressure

Outputs (1)

dd_dp

Type: DerDensityByPressure (s²/m²)

Description: Derivative of density of water at the boiling point

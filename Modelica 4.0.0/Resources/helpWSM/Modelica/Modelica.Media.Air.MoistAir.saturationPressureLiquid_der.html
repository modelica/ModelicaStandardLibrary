saturationPressureLiquid_der

Derivative function for 'saturationPressureLiquid'

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

Saturation pressure of water above the triple point temperature is computed from temperature.

Source: A Saul, W Wagner: "International equations for the saturation properties of ordinary water substance", equation 2.1

Syntax

psat_der = saturationPressureLiquid_der(Tsat, dTsat)

Inputs (2)

Tsat

Type: Temperature (K)

Description: Saturation temperature
dTsat

Type: Real (K/s)

Description: Saturation temperature derivative

Outputs (1)

psat_der

Type: Real (Pa/s)

Description: Saturation pressure derivative

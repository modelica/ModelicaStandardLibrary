K_CoupledInductors

Inductive coupling via coupling factor

Information

This information is part of the Modelica Standard Library maintained by the Modelica Association.

K_CoupledInductors is a component that allows the coupling of two inductors. k is the coefficient of coupling which must be in range [0,1].

The usage is demonstrated in the example CoupledInductors.

Parameters (1)

k

Value:

Type: Real

Description: Coupling factor

Connectors (2)

inductiveCouplePin1

Type: InductiveCouplePinIn

Description: Couple pin for inductances
